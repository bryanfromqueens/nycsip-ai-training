11:00 – 11:20 Pain Point MADLIB

Name Your Pain Point

Fill it in. Write your real one, not a tidy one. Three minutes alone, then share with your team.

My pain point at work is that .

If I had ,
I would .

Strong example: "I have to answer hundreds of visitor calls and emails all day / answer even half of those automatically / get 5 hours back a week to work on more important museum work."

Your PITA

Find one PITA outside work, and one at your internship

A PITA is a task you repeat that is a pain to do. Copy the worksheet below into a Google Doc and fill it out once for each. The Three Filters at the bottom tell you whether it is a good fit for AI.

Three Filters
FilterAskRule of thumb
ProcedureCan you write it down before you do it?Procedure = AI candidate. "It depends" = human judgment.
RepetitionDoes it repeat the same structure every time?Same shape is easier to automate.
RiskWhat is the cost if it is wrong?Low stakes: automate. High stakes: AI drafts, you decide.
The goal is to find the automation layer within a human-led process. Never automate the whole task.
11:20 – 11:50 Prompting Framework
StepWhat to doExample
01 Role & GoalTell AI who it is and what to accomplish."You are an onboarding assistant for a new intern. Help me understand my role."
02 StepsBreak the task into clear numbered steps."Step 1: ask what I do. Step 2: list what I should learn first. Step 3: draft a plan."
03 OutputDescribe exactly what you want back."Give me a draft email I can send to my team today."
04 ReflectionAsk AI to verify its own work."Confirm the answer is specific to my organization, not generic."
